About Me

My name is Muhammad Husnain Zafar. I am not someone who speaks much, but I notice everything. The pauses in conversations, the things people hide, the emotions behind ordinary words — these are the things that shape my world.

Writing is not expression for me. It is observation translated into silence. I don’t create stories — I uncover them from reality.
